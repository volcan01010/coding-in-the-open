### Some notes + and bullets +[links](https://bgs.ac.uk)

Headwinds

Going open is hindered by: previous investment and skills in proprietary technology; siloed confidential data; and requirements of security standard certifications.

Tailwinds

Going open is helped by: projects with external partners; scientists/analysts adopting reproducible workflows; Innovation Flexible Funding and supportive senior management.
