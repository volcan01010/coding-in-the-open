Going Open is a Process

Dr John A Stevenson / @volcan01010

About BGS

Headwinds

Going fully open is hindered by: previous investment and skills in proprietary technology; storage of confidential data; and requirements of security standard certifications.

Tailwinds

Going fully open is helped by: projects with external partners; scientists/analysts adopting reproducible workflows; and supportive senior management.
